Bio

The Washington Real Estate market is distinctive. Each Washington neighborhood has its own personality that reflects the history of its homes. Throughout the past century the City has gone through many transitions. Now home buyers are rediscovering the charm and history that makes living in the city so attractive.
My name is Michael Seay and I am a native Washingtonian. My great grandfather and his brother entered the burgeoning community building business in 1912. The W.C. & A.N. Miller Company built their first homes in the Petworth neighborhood and went on to build homes in Cleveland Park, Wesley Heights and Spring Valley.

The Miller family became known for quality and created communities that are considered some of the most beautiful in the country.
Choosing a real estate professional is an important decision. Every client I work with, whether buying or selling a home, benefits from my unique historical understanding of Washington Real Estate.
Since 1912 the W.C. & A.N. Miller Company has been synonymous with trust, honesty, integrity and professionalism. Today the legacy of the Miller brothers continues.
